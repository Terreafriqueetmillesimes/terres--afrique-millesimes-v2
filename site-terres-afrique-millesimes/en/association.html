Our mission

Transmit, accompany, uplift

Les Jardins de l'Espoir by Queen Egome act in France and in Africa alongside those whom life has isolated or steered off course. Our work is structured around education, health and nutrition, for the benefit of women, men and children.

Youth

Support and reintegration of young people who have dropped out of education: guidance, vocational training, and a pathway back to employment.

Elders

Dignity and social connection for isolated elderly people — visits, presence, shared moments.

Education & Nutrition

Concrete actions in education, health and access to dignified nutrition, in France and in Africa.

Every bottle counts
